About

I was born and raised in Napoleon, Ohio. So far, I've lived my entire life here outside of long trips to other states.

Sports

I've played several sports throughout my life. Soccer and baseball I both enjoyed (Although I was only good at one of those...). But my true passion for thirteen years now has been martial arts. I stand currently at a third-degree black belt. This marks me as being capable of being a teacher of the art now myself if I wish. The style I study is known as Hapkido, a Korean art.

Hobbies

I often play video games in my free time. But, I also love to read and write. Fantasy or Sci-Fi mostly, but many genres can catch my eye. I'm currently learning GODOT, a software often used to make indie games.

Favorite Book Series

Red Rising

In a color-coded society, a young Red named Darrow realizes the truth of his existence. He chooses to rise up and try to dismantle the Society that oppresses all but Gold.

Artemis Fowl

A young irish heir to a criminal empire discovers fairies. Using keen intellect and ruthless tactics he aims to steal their gold for his own goals, saving his missing father.

The Reckoners

Years ago a red star appeared in the sky, Calamity. Some people became Epics, people with incredible superpowers. This isn't a story about heroes, every Epic seems filled with power fantasy and lust to hurt others. The Reckoners work to kill Epics and restore mankind to some semblance of itself.

The Scythe Trilogy

The cloud, the sum of all human knowledge, evolved into the Thunderhead. Death is no more, no pain or suffering. Still, the Earth has limits to its resources. Scythes are specialized humans sanctioned to kill others, only a death via Scythe cannot be undone. But the world teeters on the brink of chaos as the population continues to balloon.

The Apprentice

A world where magic determines what demonic pet you can control a peasant boy is recruited to the academy. The first time in history, as magic is related to blood and all the noble families already are known. He works hard to avoid scrutiny, evolve his demonic salamander, and avoid calamity as he befriends dwarves and other "unfavorable" while the orc threat continues to encroach on the kingdom.
